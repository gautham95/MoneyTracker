📱 Install as a Phone App (PWA)

For permanent, stable storage — host this file once and add it to your home screen. Data then lives in the browser's permanent storage (not wiped on close).

Option A — Free hosting with Netlify Drop (easiest)
1
Go to netlify.com/drop on your computer
2
Drag and drop this MoneyTracker-Final.html file onto the page
3
Netlify gives you a free URL like https://random-name.netlify.app
4
Open that URL on your phone in Safari or Chrome
5
iOS: Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"  |  Android: Menu → "Add to Home Screen"
6
It opens full-screen like a native app. Data is now permanently stored in that browser's storage — not wiped on close.
Option B — GitHub Pages (also free, more permanent)
1
Create a free account at github.com
2
Create a new repository, upload this HTML file, rename it to index.html
3
Go to Settings → Pages → Enable GitHub Pages
4
Your app lives at https://yourusername.github.io/repo-name — open on phone and add to home screen
⚠️ Recommendation: Use Google Drive Sync (above) in addition to PWA hosting. That way even if you clear your browser, your data is safely backed up to Drive.
